* @appservice:libera.chat
on matrix, create a discussion with @appservice:libera.chat

then you can run commands to control your integration with irc.libera.chat.

Here are useful commands:
 - !help
 - !join
 - !nick
 - !username
 - !storepass
 - !reconnect
