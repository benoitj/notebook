#+title: sound proofing a room

* STC

stc is a measure of sound absobtion

40: minimum. regular speach mumble, but loud speech audible
50: load speach reduced
60>: loud noise reduced to minimum

* ceiling

this is >60 STC and also blocks vibration
- r12 fiberglass (or sound insulation like rockwool)
- resilient channels
- 5/8 drywall (not the light)
- green glue compound
- 5/8 drywall (not the light)


* wall

regular stud wall: stc 35
with insulation: stc 40
double sheet both sides 1/2: 46
double sheets both sides 5/8: ~50
double wall with double outer sheets and no inner sheets: ~60

stud at 24" on center adds

w/ green glue accoustic
