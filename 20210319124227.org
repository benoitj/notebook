#+title: RSS reddit

Reddit support RSS by default. Just add /.rss/ to any redit urls.

Standard ones:
 - Frontpage: https://reddit.com/.rss
 - New: https://reddit.com/new/.rss
 - Hot: https://reddit.com/hot/.rss
 - Top: https://reddit.com/top/.rss
 - Rising: https://reddit.com/rising/.rss
 - Controversial: https://reddit.com/controversial/.rss

You can also add dates, ex: https://reddit.com/top/?sort=top&t=month/.rss
Where t can be hour, day, week, month, year, all
