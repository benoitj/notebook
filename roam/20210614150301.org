#+title: definition of ready -- DoR

* prioritized using [[file:20210614150450.org][MoSCow prioritization method]]
* Adhere to [[file:20210614150814.org][SMART Criteria]]
* Adhere to [[file:20210614151058.org][INVEST mnemonic]]
* has clearly defined acceptance criteria
* has estimated value
* team (PO included) has reviewed and accepts it
