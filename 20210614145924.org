#+title: definition of done -- DoD

* story level
** kicked off by the PO, QA, and developers
** test scenarios defined
** build green
** CI
** peer reviewed (imply quality verified)
** desk checked
** QA testing passed
* iteration level
** Interation Demo
** Accepted by the PO and team as done
* release level
** regression tested
** included in release notes
** pass UAT testing
