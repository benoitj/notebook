#+title: scotch whisky


* single malt

Comes from one single barrel.

Divided in 6 regions below:

** Speyside

- Largest scotch producing (volumes), lots of producers.
- Access to fresh water.
- nut / floral / fruity taste
- smooth
- not smoke due to no peat

some producers:
- Balvenie
  - 14y Carrabian cask?
- Glenfiddich

** Highlands

- largest region (geography)
- lots of various taste due to geo
  + north: brown / smoke / peat
  + south: smother

some producers:
- Oban
  - smoke / Peat
- Glenmorangie
  - Port Cask?
  - Smooth

** Lowlands
- right above england
- easy to drink/start
- Smooth / sweet

some producers:
- glenkinchie
- Auchentoshan

** Islay

- Peat / smoke taste
- flavorful
- not for everybody

some producers:
- Laphroaig
- Lagavulin

** Island
- no longer an official region
- lots of different taste
- smooth / earth / peat balance

some producers:
- highland park
- Talesker

** Campbeltown

- small region
- hard to find
- whool / sea

* Blended scotch

mix from different barrels / age

some producers
- Shivas regal
- Sheep dip
- Johnny walker
