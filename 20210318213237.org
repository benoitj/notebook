#+BEGIN_SRC sh :noeval
cat /proc/15439/smaps | grep -i pss |  awk '{Total+=$2} END {print Total/1024" MB"}'

RSS represent close to reality, but

** better measure with /proc

#+BEGIN_SRC sh :noeval
PID=10 cat /proc/$PID/smaps | grep -i pss |  awk '{Total+=$2} END {print Total/1024" MB"}'
#+END_SRC
